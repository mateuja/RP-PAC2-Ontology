IfBookingIncludesTripAndFlightThenTripHasFlightback to ToC

If a booking includes a trip and a flight, then the trip has the flight

includesTrip op(?,?) , Booking c(?) , Flight c(?) , includesFlight op(?,?) , Trip c(?) -> hasFlight op(?,?)

IfFlightHasPassengerAndPassengerHasBookingThenBookingIncludesFlightback to ToC

If a flight has passenger onboard and it has a booking, then that booking includes the flight

Booking c(?) , Flight c(?) , hasOnboard op(?,?) , hasBooking op(?,?) , Passenger c(?) -> includesFlight op(?,?)
